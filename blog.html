The Daileigh

The Daileigh’s Ashleigh Hutchinson advises her readers to choose their ideal wardrobe. She aspires to help women of all ages create or find a look they love. Helping older women feel confident in their style is something Ashleigh is particularly interested in. She wants to refute the idea that particular trends are exclusively suited for a particular age group. At the basis of Ashleigh’s mindset is the idea that age is just a number Ashleigh’s blog is full of advice on how to build a capsule wardrobe or how to dress better with the clothing you already own for ordinary ladies. She has authored e-books, and blog posts, and even presented online webinars to help people improve their sense of style.
